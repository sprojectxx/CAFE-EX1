Our Story

A space crafted
for slow mornings.

Ombra was born from a love of light, texture, and the unhurried pace of a good cup. Every corner was designed to be a moment — somewhere to breathe, read, or simply be.

We source single-origin beans from small farms, pulled by hands that believe coffee is a ritual worth honouring.
